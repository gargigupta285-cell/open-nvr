# OpenNVR Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

OpenNVR is dual-licensed (AGPL-3.0-or-later + a commercial license —
see [LICENSING.md](LICENSING.md)). That model only works if the project
holds the rights to offer every contributed line under both licenses.
This CLA is how contributors grant those rights **while keeping their
own copyright**.

By submitting a contribution to the AGPL-licensed components of this
repository, you agree that:

1. **You keep your copyright.** Nothing here transfers ownership.
2. **Grant of rights.** You grant the OpenNVR project a perpetual,
   worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable license to
   reproduce, prepare derivative works of, publicly display, publicly
   perform, sublicense, and distribute your contribution — **including
   the right to license it under terms other than the AGPL** (this is
   what enables the commercial edition).
3. **Patent grant.** You grant a patent license for claims necessarily
   infringed by your contribution alone or combined with the project,
   on Apache-2.0 §3 terms.
4. **You have the right to contribute it.** The work is yours, or you
   have permission from your employer/rights-holder to submit it under
   this CLA.
5. **No warranty.** Contributions are provided as-is.

**How to sign:** the CLA bot on pull requests records your agreement
once per contributor (individual) or via a signed entity form for
employer-owned contributions. Contributions to the Apache-2.0 SDKs
(`sdk/`, adapter contract) do not require the CLA — a DCO
`Signed-off-by` line suffices, since Apache-2.0 inbound = outbound
already permits everything the project needs.

*Based on the Apache Software Foundation ICLA structure. Not legal
advice; the executed agreement is the operative text.*
